--## DotBox V1.0 ##--
Made in 1 week for fun

--## HOW TO OPEN ##--
Simply double-click the game.exe file, and it will launch!

NOTE: Your antivirus may flag this file as a virus. It is NOT a virus.
Safety certificates cost money, and I don't have any.

--## HOW TO PLAY ##--
You have three different tools to choose from.

1. Add tool
Left click anywhere to add dots
Right click on a dot to remove it

2. Move tool
Left click and hold a dot to move it around
It will inherit the speed that you release it at
Right click a dot to toggle freezing it

3. Paint tool
Left click a dot to paint it
Left click on the color wheel to pick a color
Right click a dot to pick its color
